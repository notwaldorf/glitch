Hearing Vera Molnár

Vera Molnár is one of the earliest pioneers of digital art. In Un Deux Trois, she draws 3 kinds of lines, each in its own third of the canvas.

In this demo, you will hear a musical trio generated with Magenta.js. Each of the 3 instruments represents one of the 3 types of lines. As the music progresses, the painting comes to life.
