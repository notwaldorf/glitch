Why This Matters
in the Age of AI
🤖AI can generate images, write code, and compose music on demand
📊AI is great at the average. You are the only one with your specific voice.
🎯Deciding which of 500 outputs feels right — that's taste. No model has it for you.
👩‍🏫Students who make intentional creative choices with code are building the skill AI can't replicate
